Mission Statement

And now saith The Lord that formed me from the womb to be His servant, to bring Jacob back again to Him, though Israel be not gathered, yet shall I be glorious in His sight. And He siath, it is a light thing that thou shouldest be my servant, to raise up the tribes of Jacob and to restore the preserved of Israel, I will also give thee for a ight unto the gentiles that thou mayest be my salvation unto the ends of the earth. - Isaiah 49:5,6

Vision

1. Renewal or regeneration of millions of nominal and unregenerated church people.
2. Giving light that leads to salvation to sinners in all other quarters, than the Christian religious quarters.
3. Ensuring oneness and restoration to the continually disintegrating body of Christ, the Church.
4. Healing the sick, restoring the disabled, delivering the possessed and oppressed by the devils.
5. Warning all that will remain adamant and be lost eventually and eternally.
